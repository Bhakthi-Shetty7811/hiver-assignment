Title: Configuring SLAs for Your Shared Inboxes
Summary: SLAs help set response and resolution expectations for customer queries.

Steps:
1. Open Admin Panel → SLAs.
2. Click Create SLA Policy.
3. Define first-response and resolution time for each customer tier.
4. Choose which inbox the SLA applies to.
5. Save the SLA.

Notes:
• SLAs apply only to new conversations.
• You can override SLA times for specific tags.
