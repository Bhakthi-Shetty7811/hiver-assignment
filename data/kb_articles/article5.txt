Title: How to Send Bulk Emails Using Mail Merge
Summary: Mail Merge allows you to send personalized emails at scale.

Steps:
1. Go to Mail Merge.
2. Upload a CSV file with customer data.
3. Pick an email template.
4. Customize fields like {{name}} or {{company}}.
5. Schedule or send immediately.

Notes:
• Avoid special characters in CSV headers.
• Open-rate tracking is available.
